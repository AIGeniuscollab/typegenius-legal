Privacy Policy

TypeGenius AI Keyboard

Last Updated: June 15, 2025

At TypeGenius, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered keyboard application.

1. Information We Collect

TypeGenius collects the following types of information:

  • Text Input: When you use AI features (voice transcription, grammar correction, text enhancement), we temporarily process your text to provide the service.
  • Voice Data: When you use voice-to-text features, audio recordings are sent to our servers for transcription and are immediately deleted after processing.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance and features.
  • Device Information: Basic device information such as OS version and device type to ensure compatibility.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide AI-powered keyboard features (transcription, grammar correction, text enhancement)
  • Improve and optimize our services
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Analyze usage patterns to develop new features

3. Data Storage and Security

  • No Long-Term Storage: Your typed text and voice recordings are processed in real-time and are NOT stored on our servers after processing.
  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols.
  • Third-Party AI Services: We use OpenAI's GPT and Whisper APIs to power our AI features. Your data is processed according to OpenAI's data usage policies.
  • Local Preferences: Your keyboard settings (dark theme, key preview) are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • AI Service Providers: We use OpenAI's API services to provide AI features. Your text is processed according to their privacy policy.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Opt-Out: You can choose not to use AI features at any time by simply not activating them.
  • Data Deletion: Since we don't store your text or voice data long-term, there's no persistent data to delete.
  • Permissions: You can revoke microphone and other permissions through your device settings at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

TypeGenius is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: For AI text enhancement and voice transcription. OpenAI Privacy Policy

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of TypeGenius after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. International Users

TypeGenius is available globally. By using our service, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy, regardless of where you are located.

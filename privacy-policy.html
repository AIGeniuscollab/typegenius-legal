Privacy Policy

TypeGenius AI Keyboard

Effective Date: February 1, 2026 | Last Updated: February 26, 2026

At TypeGenius ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our TypeGenius AI Keyboard mobile application (the "App").

Key Points:
  • We do NOT store your typed text or voice recordings after processing
  • We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do NOT use your data for advertising purposes
  • All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols

1. How TypeGenius Works

TypeGenius functions as an Input Method Editor (IME) on your device. This means:

  • Local Processing: Standard typing (without AI features) is processed entirely on your device. We do NOT transmit your keystrokes to any server during normal typing.
  • AI Feature Processing: When you explicitly activate AI features (voice transcription, grammar correction, text enhancement, Genius), only that specific text is securely transmitted to our servers for processing.
  • No Keystroke Logging: We do NOT log, store, or analyze your typing patterns, passwords, or any text you type unless you explicitly use an AI feature.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Text for AI Processing: When you use AI features (voice-to-text, grammar correction, text enhancement, translation, Genius), we temporarily process the text you submit.
  • Genius Instructions and Context: When you use the Genius feature, we process the instruction you type and, if you have selected text, the selected text you choose to include as context. Both are sent to our servers and third-party AI providers for processing and are not retained afterward.
  • Voice Recordings: When you use voice transcription, audio is transmitted to our servers, processed, and immediately deleted.
  • Feedback Submissions: If you use our in-app "Help Us Improve" feedback form, we collect the feedback responses you provide (what you love, what could be better, feature requests). This information is stored in our database to help us improve the App.
  • Email Address (Optional): You may optionally provide your email address when submitting feedback, if you would like us to follow up. We will only use this email to respond to your specific feedback and for no other purpose.
  • Support Communications: If you contact us for support, we may collect your email address and the content of your communications.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Instance Identifier: A randomly generated UUID to manage your subscription and daily usage limits. This identifier is NOT linked to your device hardware, personal identity, or any account. It is stored on our server alongside usage records and feedback submissions to associate them with your app installation.
  • Usage Counts: We track the number of times you use each AI feature (voice transcription, grammar correction, rephrasing, tone adjustment, summarization, Genius) to enforce daily usage limits. We do NOT store the content of your text.
  • Subscription Status: Whether you have an active Pro subscription, to determine your usage limits.
  • Device Information (Feedback Only): If you submit feedback through the in-app form, basic device information (device name, device model, Android version) is included alongside your feedback to help us diagnose issues. This information is only collected when you actively submit the feedback form and is NOT collected during normal keyboard use.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Device hardware identifiers (IMEI, serial number, etc.)
  • Passwords or sensitive credentials
  • Credit card or payment information (handled by Google Play)
  • Precise location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos, files, or other media
  • Browsing history
  • Crash reports or diagnostics
  • Text typed using standard keyboard (without AI features)

4. Data Retention

  • Text and Voice Data: Processed in real-time and deleted immediately after processing. We do NOT retain copies.
  • Genius Instructions and Context: Processed in real-time and deleted immediately after the AI response is generated. We do NOT retain copies.
  • Usage Counts: Stored on our server; reset daily.
  • Instance Identifiers: Stored on our server for the duration of your app installation to manage usage limits and associate feedback.
  • Feedback Submissions: Stored in our database for as long as needed to improve our services. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.
  • Email Addresses (from Feedback): Retained only as long as needed to follow up on your feedback, then deleted. You may request deletion at any time.
  • Support Communications: Retained for up to 24 months to provide ongoing support.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS 1.2 or higher encryption.
  • Secure Infrastructure: Our backend services are hosted on Railway, a secure cloud platform with SOC 2 compliance.
  • Database Security: Feedback submissions and usage data are stored in a secured database with access restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Access Controls: Access to our systems is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • No Long-Term Storage of Content: Text, voice data, and Genius instructions are processed in memory and never written to persistent storage.
  • Local Data: Your keyboard preferences and settings are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

6.1 Service Providers

  • OpenAI: We use OpenAI's GPT and Whisper APIs to power AI features, including the Genius feature. Text, voice data, and Genius instructions are sent to OpenAI for processing. OpenAI's data usage is governed by their API Data Usage Policy, which states that API data is not used to train their models.
  • Railway: Our backend infrastructure provider. They do not have access to your data content.
  • Google Play: Handles all payment processing for subscriptions. We do not receive or store your payment information.

6.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users and services.
